Smash Karts is a 3D multiplayer IO kart battle game. Drive your go-kart, pick up weapons, and blow up other karts to win! Keep playing to level up and unlock new characters and prizes.

How to Play Smash Karts

Battle and earn XP

Face off with other players in the arena for 3 minutes of kart-smashing mayhem. The game features several maps to explore, and it is continually updated with new ones. Each public match you play will give you XP, which you can then use to level up. 

Leveling up will reward you with coins, hats, wheels, and character tokens. You can use character tokens in the prize machine to unlock new characters with varying rarity levels.

Pick up weapons

Drive over the boxes containing question marks to pick up random weapons and power-ups. You’ll find anything from invincibility to missiles, machine guns, and mines to use against your opponents. Get in your kart and cause some chaos!

Available Weapons

  • Rockets: fairly straightforward, helpful for hitting opponents in front of you.
  • Machine Gun: it rapidly fires several projectiles and can take down enemies around you, as long as you are close by.
  • Bullets: with these, you’ll fire a series of bullets that will go straight from your position. It can be useful to fire at enemies in crowded contexts or on long, straight alleys.
  • Mines: you can deploy three mines that serve as a static trap for your challengers. Mines are particularly effective when placed around corners or hidden behind item boxes.
  • Cannonballs: using this will send four cannonballs in the direction of your choice. Cannonballs require precision to be effective, but can eliminate multiple adversaries simultaneously.
  • Spiky-Go-Round: activating the spiky-go-round will add rotating balls with spikes to your vehicle, which will make any contact with opponents lethal for them.
  • Nuke: a very powerful missile that has a wide impact radius.
  • Lobgrenuke: with a lobgrenuke you are placing a TNT-like static bomb on the ground. It gets activated when one of your enemies passes nearby or a few seconds after deployment.
  • Snowball Blaster: fires ice at opponents and blocks them for 3 seconds in a block of ice. Touching this block while active will eliminate the kart containing it.
  • Invincibility: the most powerful of all weapons, allows you to neutralize any shot aimed at you and, at the same time, to take down any kart by simply touching it.

Customize your kart!

Choose between a selection of wacky little characters and karts. You can customize your kart and characters in the customize menu. There, you’ll find a selection of karts, characters, hats, and holiday items! Use your coins, character tokens, and gems to buy various in-game items.

Play in events

Various seasonal events occur throughout the year, so make sure to come back and play Smash Karts at special times of year for new content. New maps are available around Halloween, Christmas, and other festive occasions, and you can reap unique rewards.
